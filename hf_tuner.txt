Mounting points are in line with the shaft

Panel mount points to back: 40 mm
Mounting screw separation: 16.3 mm
Width: 24 mm
Shaft length after mounting points: 14 mm
Height: 31 mm
Shaft center above bottom: 19 mm


### 150 pF variable cap

### BNC connectors

Both shafts dia: 3 mm
Gear shaft len: 84 mm
Slider shaft len: 92 mm
Slider opposite edge to shaft center: 8 mm
Slider top edge to shaft center: 5.38 mm
Slider Shafts center-to-center for gear to work: 7.8 mm
Slider len: 50 mm
Slider shaft attachments outside-to-outside: 35 mm
Slider inside edge to shaft: 3.5 mm

Slider shaft fixed mounts inside distance: 42 mm + 35 mm + 1 mm (slider attachment flange clearence) = 78 mm

Total depth from back: 18.2 mm
